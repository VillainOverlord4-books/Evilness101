Gulmarg

Gulmarg is located in the Baramulla district of Jammu and Kashmir, Gulmarg is one of India’s most breathtaking picnic spots and tourist destinations. Nestled at an altitude of 8,694 feet, this meadow of flowers is known for its scenic beauty, winter sports, and romantic charm. The name Gulmarg means “meadow of flowers” in Persian. This picturesque town was originally called Gauri Marg and served as a favourite summer retreat for Mughal emperors, especially Sultan Yusuf Shah and later Emperor Jahangir, who collected wildflowers from the region. Apart from the natural beauty that it holds, Gulmarg is famous for offering a range of activities, be it summer or winter. As a popular summer retreat, you can come here for trekking and golfing. In winter, the place promised top adventures including Gulmarg skiing, snowboarding and sledging.

Pahalgam

You’ll surely fall in love with these Pahalgam tourist places. Pahalgam is a beautiful town located in the valleys of Kashmir. People from all around the world visit here just to experience its natural beauty and amazing tourist attractions. Let’s talk about its attractions, Betaab valley is its topmost tourist attraction famous for its green valleys and amazing views of mountains, and it also acts as a starting point of many popular treks. The Lidder River is a perfect destination to picnic with your loved ones. Sheshnag Lake is surrounded by beautiful snow-covered mountains and holds great religious importance.
